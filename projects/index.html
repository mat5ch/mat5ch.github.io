Projects

Melody Helpr

Melody Helpr is an app that tries to help with creating melodies from scratch. It is meant to be used in companion with the Open Source recording software Ardour. You can create new melody sequences, double or halve them, recreate at any point and finally transfer the created sequence from Melody Helpr back into Ardour to further work with it.

Find this project at my GitHub.

What’s that, Arcanoid?

“What’s that, Arcanoid?” is a little game made in Max/MSP. The motivation behind it was to explore the possibilities of the audio-visual programming environment. I soon came to the conclusion that I could realize a modified version of Arcanoid. From the beginning, guessing a song title was planned as the special feature of the game.

Find this project at my GitHub.

Noisey

The following song was created in the context of a lecture at university where a concept for a musical composition should be developed. There were no explicit guidelines regarding the genre and content of the composition. The only conditions given were that the resulting musical piece should last around 4 minutes and that the concept was conclusive. My concept dealt with the composition of a piece of music with only one sample, a white noise sample. As genre for the music piece, I chose the EDM (electronic dance music) genre. The song was aptly called “Noisey”.

Find the song on my SoundCloud.
